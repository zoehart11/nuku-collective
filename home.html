What is Nuku Collective?

Nuku is a collaborative dedicated to celebrating and showcasing local creatives, aiming to foster connections, conversations and collaborations Ōtepoti’s creative communities.

Nuku Collective acknowledges and respects ngā iwi Māori as the Tangata Whenua of Aotearoa and are committed to upholding the principles of the Treaty of Waitangi.
